UNICEF Drones for Sustainable Development Goals Toolkit

The UNICEF Drones for Sustainable Develop Goals Toolkit is a collection of tools curated by the Office of Innovation. The primary objective of the toolkit is to provide an easily accessible repository of drone-related open-source software, data, ai models, standards, courseware, and guides that address the SDGs. All the products in the toolkit must be open source and eligible for nomination as a Digital Public Good.
